## ✨ Features

### 1. **Hotel Management System (PMS) – Front Desk**
Manage guest check-ins, check-outs, and room assignments with ease. Our intuitive front desk module provides:
- Real-time room availability tracking
- Quick guest registration and check-in/check-out processes
- Guest profiles and history management
- Room status management
- Folio management and billing

### 2. **Channel Manager with Real-time Sync**
Manage rates and inventory across multiple OTAs and booking platforms simultaneously:
- Centralized rate management across all channels
- Real-time inventory synchronization
- Overbooking prevention
- Multi-currency support
- Automated channel updates

### 3. **Restaurant POS – Efficient Order & Billing**
Integrate in-house dining with seamless order management:
- Fast order entry and tracking
- Kitchen display system (KDS) integration
- Table management
- Itemized billing and payment processing
- Inventory tracking for food & beverages

### 4. **WhatsApp Business Integration**
Direct communication with guests at their preferred platform:
- Send booking confirmations and check-in details
- Guest support and assistance requests
- Upselling opportunities (spa, dining, activities)
- Real-time notifications and updates
- Two-way messaging for enhanced guest engagement

### 5. **Housekeeping Management – Smart Task Coordination**
Optimize your housekeeping operations with intelligent task management:
- Digital task assignment and tracking
- Real-time status updates
- Supervisor dashboards
- Quality assurance checklists
- Staff performance analytics

### 6. **Booking Engine – Seamless Direct Reservations**
Empower guests to book directly from your website:
- Responsive, mobile-friendly design
- Multiple payment options
- Guest upselling during booking
- Instant confirmation emails
- Reduced dependency on OTAs

### 7. **Payment Gateway Integration**
Secure, reliable payment processing:
- Multiple payment method support (Cards, UPI, Net Banking, etc.)
- PCI-DSS compliance
- Automated reconciliation
- Invoice management
- Secure transaction processing
